---
title: Headings
---

Apply the style of a semantic header element to any other element. Looks like a
header visually, but carries none of the semantic value.

<p class="h1">.h1</p>
<p class="h2">.h2</p>
<p class="h3">.h3</p>
<p class="h4">.h4</p>
<p class="h5">.h5</p>
<p class="h6">.h6</p>
